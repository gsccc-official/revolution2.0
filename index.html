How to Register

Follow these simple steps to complete your registration for Revolution 2.0.

01

Fill in Your Details

Go to the Register page. Enter your full name, email, phone number, date of birth, institution, and class/year. All fields marked with * are required.

02

Choose Your Segment(s)

Select one or more segments you want to participate in. Each segment costs ৳50. If you choose Singing (Bijoyer Shur), you must also select a Category — A (Class 8–10) or B (Class 11–12 / HSC Batch 26).

The total amount you need to pay will be shown automatically based on how many segments you select.
03

Pay via bKash

Send the total amount via bKash Send Money to 01811221844 (Personal). Use your full name as the reference/remarks.

Fee is ৳50 per segment. If you selected 3 segments, pay ৳150 in a single transaction.
04

Enter Transaction Details

After paying, enter your bKash Transaction ID (TrxID) and the bKash number you paid from in the form.

05

Upload Payment Screenshot

Take a screenshot of your bKash payment confirmation and upload it. This is mandatory for verification.

06

Submit & Download Invoice

Click Submit Registration. On success, click View & Download Invoice to get your payment receipt. Save it for future reference — you may need it on event day.

You will receive a confirmation email within 48 hours of approval.
